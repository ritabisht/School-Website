The Chairman Desk (Sh. Umesh Mahajan)

Education, in the largest sense, is any act or experience that has a formative effect on the mind, character or physical ability of an individual. In its technical sense, education is the process by which society deliberately transmits its accumulated knowledge, skills and values from one generation to another. Education facilitates development of the brain,maturing a young adult into an able, dependent adult. Education is a tool that so many people lack and such people end up regretting not having acquired it. School provides a place to acquire education as well as to push yourself to see your full potential. School also is a place where hard work pays off and it is evident in grades Our school has an extremely unswerving, devoted and qualified faculty that constantly strives to make learning a pleasurable experience for children. As a parent, you want the best for your child and we are proud to be working with you to ensure that they are given the right knowledge to face future challenges. I would like to acknowledge the role being played by the committed and dedicated team of our teachers. We hope to create an atmosphere of reverence for education and a healthy environment where work, sports and co-curricular activities will mould our students and spur them on to be the brightest and the best. Discipline, courage and perseverance are instilled among the children through a wide range of games and sports activities based on their needs and interests. Competitive sports, which are an integral part of the school curriculum, not only motivate the children but also help in developing sportsmanship, tolerance and teamwork. Students learn the value of work ethic and responsibility. The curriculum of our school is research oriented and based on teamwork vis-à-vis discussions, group project work, discovery and exploration. Various combinations of subjects are offered so that students can pursue their desired courses of study later in life. A school Magazine is a mirror and index of the activities of an Institution and all round development of the students.Every year, we get the school magazine ( VIDYA DEEP) published, and the same is distributed to all the students without taking any extra charge from them. The doors of the School are always open for the students and their parents for any suggestion or guidance.
