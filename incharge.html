The Secondry Incharge Desk(Geeta Mahajan)

Education is a step today for a better and a resourceful tomorrow. To be educated is being synonymous to being respected. Education makes people easy to lead but difficult to drive, easy to govern but impossible to enslave. Education is important for everyone because it empowers people with knowledge, skills and self confidence which are necessary to participate fully in the development process. Our school has adopted CCE system this year, which helps in improving student's performance by identifying his / her learning difficulties at regular time intervals, right from the beginning of the academic session and implying suitable remedial measures for enhancing their learning performance. As a part of this new system, student's marks are replaced by grades which are evaluated through a series of curricular and extracurricular activities. Academic grades are also awarded to students based on work experience skills, innovation team work, behavior etc. to evaluate and present an overall measure of the student's ability. I have spent my formative years under the mantle of loving care of Hans Raj Smarak School. I have learnt the most valuable lessons of my life from my seniors and colleagues. Being a part of this school, I feel fortunate and proud. I hope and pray that our school may continue to rise with the same gusto with student ever alive and vibrant and ready to make the world a better place.
